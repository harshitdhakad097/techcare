Frequently Asked Questions

How long does a typical repair take?

Most repairs take 1-3 hours depending on the issue. Complex problems may take longer, and we'll provide an estimate upfront.

Do you offer remote support?

Yes! We offer 24/7 remote support for most issues. We'll guide you through fixing your problem or take control to resolve it faster.

Is my data safe with you?

Absolutely! We follow strict data privacy protocols and never access personal files unless necessary. All work is backed by a service warranty.

What areas do you serve?

We provide remote support nationwide and on-site support in metro areas. Contact us to check if we serve your location.

Can you fix other devices besides PC?

We specialize in PC and laptop support. For other devices, feel free to contact us - we may be able to help!

Do you provide follow-up support?

Yes! Our Standard and Premium plans include follow-up support. We'll make sure the issue is completely resolved.
